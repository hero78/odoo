What is CinetPay?

CinetPay is a payment gateway that operates in 10+ countries. It helps businesses accept payment online, in-store, and over phone modes. It offers secure, reliable payment processing, easy integration, and 24x7 support. It provides solutions for all businesses of all sizes, making it an ideal choice for companies seeking an affordable, quick settlement, and PCI DSS-compliant payment solution.

You can use it in 40 mobile and credit card payment methods for accepting payments from a wide range of customers. It's a certified PCI, CSP, DSS, and 3D secure. Moreover, it allows you to track all your transactions 24x7.
